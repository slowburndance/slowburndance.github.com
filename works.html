Half Body at Once

Premieres October 16

Half Body at Once is a three part contemporary dance film work by SLOWBURN Dance Company. This work revels in impermanent movement, making a home on a temporary frontier. Interim versions of the self wander along an empty road, in search of something that is not yet concrete.

She buys clothes for a shape that's still forming.

One half of a boat is sailing at full speed.
