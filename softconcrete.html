Soft Concrete

Soft Concrete is a site-specific performance series, curated and produced by Meredith Pellon in partnership with Jack Apartments. In each installment of this series, selected choreographers create new dance works in response to research questions that investigate movement concepts such as juxtaposition of texture and the qualities of softness.

Soft Concrete III will take place on August 6, 2020. Applications are open now and the deadline is March 15 at 11pm. Selected choreographers will be asked to create new works that are 9-12 minutes in length. These works must be inspired by the following questions:

How do we absorb rigidity?

Where does our malleability end?

What does lightness in movement look like?
