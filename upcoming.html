November 15th, 8pm

Soft Concrete II at 1427 11th Ave, Seattle WA in partnership with Jack Apartments

Soft Concrete is a site-specific performance series, curated by Meredith Pellon in partnership with Jack Apartments. In Soft Concrete II, choreographers are asked to create new dance works that investigate the following questions:

  • How can physical texture inform movement?
  • What are different ways in which we can soften?
  • How can warmth be found in structure?
