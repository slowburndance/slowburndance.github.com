Audition



SLOWBURN Dance is hiring 1-2 dancers to join our company! We are looking for professional dancers ages 18 or older who are skilled in contemporary dance and improvisation.

Auditions will be held in Oakland studio at NOD (1621 12th Ave, Seattle, WA 98122) from 1:30pm - 3:00pm on Saturday, August 10th.

All dancers must pre-register in order to attend the audition. The deadline for registration is August 8, 2024. To register, read through this PDF of more detailed information and then fill out this form.
